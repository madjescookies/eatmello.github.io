OUR MISSION

Our mission is to bring joy, awaken curiosity, and take all of you on a flavorful journey, one sweet bite at a time!

What better way to do that than with delicious cookies that are guaranteed to titillate your tastebuds? Because, let's be honest, you've never felt sad while eating a tasty treat, and if you have, it was probably one of those fake keto things. No shade to the keto community, we love butter and coconut oil too.

We want to add more exciting choices to your grocery store’s cookie aisle and honor those relaxing moments where all you can handle is your favorite show/book and a delicious cookie. Sometimes you may want to share us with friends – we’re okay with that too, we want you to be the talk of the party ;)

Gone are the days of shamefully eating an entire box of cookies in the dark because you’ve been too strict on yourself. We’re here to remind you that it’s okay to treat yourself! Have a bit of happiness. You deserve it!

OUR FLAVORS

Each of our flavors puts a twist on the classics by using interesting spices that feature different regions of the world.
For the less adventurous, we've brought you the classics 2.0 – amped up in taste and satisfaction.

MEET MADJÉ

Madjé grew up baking (and eating) French pastries in Senegal. She moved to the USA to pursue a degree architecture, and was planning on getting a fashion design masters, but eventually found herself working as a software engineer.
She enjoys making up recipes and blending traditional american flavors with those of the various cultures she has been exposed to throughout her childhood. She has brought that creativity to her baking and after sharing unconventional and exciting treats with her friends and family, she decided to expand her audience and add more fun to your grocery store's cookie aisle!
